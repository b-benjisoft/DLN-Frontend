DATALESS NETWORK

Sign up now and recieve beta access before the service releases

People lack access to the internet due to everything from running out of mobile data to government censorship. Dataless Network aims to combat this by using SMS messaging to serve internet content. We'll be getting information from Wikipedia, Google Translate, Transport API, and many other sources. This will allow users to get the information they need, as intuitively and as quickly as possible. The service isn't released publicly quite yet, however if you register your interest by inputting your email, you'll be the first to know about it's release.
